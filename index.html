Why have a tour guide?

While you sometimes may prefer to venture out on your own when vacationing or visiting friends and family there are benefits to having a more experienced, knowledgeable and entertaining tour guide.

Local Knowledge

Our reputable tour guides are recruited from within the local community, so they have a good knowledge of the area's history and culture. They are able to provide up-to-date factual information and answer any questions that you may have about the area. Plus, you'll get great insider tips on restaurants, nightlife, and shopping.

Organization

A guided tour handles all the organization and prep work for visiting landmarks in the area. You save time and energy that you would normally put into planning out your own tours. This can be especially beneficial when you have very little time.

Socialization

Joining a group of others adds a social aspect to the tour. Experiencing a new city with others allows you to share your reactions and connect with other group members.

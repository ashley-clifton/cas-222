Landmarks Tour

This tour will help you cement your Portland knowledge and cover all of Portland's famous landmarks and must see spots. We use a spacious shuttle bus to travel around Portland's quadrants and famous bridges. When you finish the tour, you can tell everyone that you've seen it all in Portland.

Details

  • Duration: 4 Hours
  • Transportation: Walking and Driving
  • Food: Included
  • Price: $110

Included

  • Complimentary tour booklet with photos and brief descriptions of the locations we visit
  • Complimentary local snacks

Tips for a Great Tour

  • Make reservations early
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Wear comfortable clothes that are suitable for walking
  • Dress prepared for changes in weather including an all-weather jacket
  • Bring sunscreen to protect your skin
  • Bring sunglasses to protect your eyes from UV exposure
  • Bring a camera with extra batteries
  • Notify your guide before the tour of any special needs that you may have for anyone in your group
  • Arrive 15 minutes before the start of the tour
